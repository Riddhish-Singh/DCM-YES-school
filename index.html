Frequently Asked Questions – DCM YES School

Q1: What is the vision of DCM YES School?

Founded in 1946 by Late Shri M. R. Dass, our vision is “From Cradle to Corporate,” which means nurturing students from early years to global career readiness. We blend traditional values with future-facing technologies like AI, AR, VR, and ML.

Q2: When do admissions open and how do I apply?

Admissions begin every November for the new academic session. For detailed information on eligibility, required documents, and key dates, please visit the admissions section on our official website.

Q3: What curriculum does the school follow?

We follow the CBSE curriculum, enhanced with proprietary programs like Mother’s Lap, CCLICK, SLITEE, and CLASSE, ensuring holistic academic, emotional, and social development.

Q4: What facilities are available on campus?
  • Smart Classrooms with interactive tech
  • Advanced labs for AI, Robotics, Sciences, and more
  • Modern Library (Print + Digital)
  • Expansive sports infrastructure with trained coaches
  • Etiquette Villa to foster soft skills and leadership
  • Amusement Zone for early learners
Q5: Does the school offer international programs?

Yes, students can opt for a Dual Diploma through our partnership with Houghton Academy, New York, enabling them to earn both Indian and U.S. high school credentials.

Q6: What is the “Earn While You Learn” program?

This initiative provides students with internship opportunities during school, helping them gain real-world professional experience early on.

Q7: How does the school support entrepreneurship?

Our Entrepreneurial Development Platform empowers students to ideate, build, and present startups through mentorship, incubation, and exposure to global events.

Q9: What security measures are in place on campus?

DCM YES School is under 24/7 CCTV surveillance and staffed by trained security professionals, ensuring a safe and emotionally secure environment for students.

Q10: How does the school promote health and wellness?

We offer a fully-equipped health clinic, regular health check-ups, mental wellness workshops, and nutrition guidance to ensure physical and mental well-being.

Q11: What is Yespreneurship?

Yespreneurship is our unique entrepreneurship initiative where students learn business planning, pitch ideas, and connect with real-world mentors and investors — all while in school.

Q12: How is technology integrated into student learning?

Students actively learn and use technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Augmented Reality, and Virtual Reality to solve real-world problems.

Q13: What sports are offered at the school?

We offer structured training in various sports including football, cricket, basketball, and athletics. Our school has spacious sports grounds and expert coaches to ensure a healthy balance of academics and physical fitness.

Q14: How does the school prepare students to be global citizens?

Through global exchange programs, partnerships, and participation in international conferences, students develop cross-cultural understanding and leadership skills.
